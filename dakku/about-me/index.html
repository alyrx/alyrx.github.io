Biography

Gabriel Bravo, artistically recognized as Dakku, is a prominent Colombian music producer, born on February 16, 2006. His inspiration comes from outstanding figures in electronic music such as Geoxor, SVRGE, Kotori, Porter Robinson, Skrillex and Virtual Riot.

Dakku's music, as well as his covers, are infused with the anime style and characterized by the inclusion of vocal chops, elements that have contributed to consolidating his name in the industry.


At the age of 16, he debuted with two notable singles: "Anyway" (160,000+ views) and "Healing" (300,000+ views). However, it was at the age of 17 that he achieved his most outstanding achievement with the release of "Ephemeral", which accumulated 520,000+ views.
This achievent radically transformed the perception of Dakku, positioning him as a constantly evolving innovator in the electronic music scene and cementing his influence in the artistic field.
